About Me

Welcome to my page! I’m Damien Tello, a seasoned server with over 20 years of experience in the hospitality industry. My journey in this field began with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences and has evolved into a deep expertise in wine and liquor pairing.

Over the years, I’ve had the pleasure of working in various high-end restaurants and bars, where I’ve honed my skills in understanding and anticipating guest needs. My extensive knowledge of wine varietals, regions, and tasting notes, combined with a keen sense for the nuances of liquor and mixology, allows me to curate exceptional pairings that elevate any meal.

I pride myself on my ability to manage high-volume environments with efficiency and grace, always maintaining a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Excellent communication, problem-solving, and teamwork are at the heart of my service philosophy, and I strive to bring these qualities to every interaction.

In addition to my hospitality expertise, I am a self-taught programmer and website designer, dedicated to helping small businesses thrive. Leveraging the knowledge I’ve gained in the restaurant industry, particularly in hospitality and management, I apply these principles to my work with small businesses, helping them create a strong online presence and make a mark in their respective markets.

I am constantly learning new computer languages to enhance my overall ability and to utilize my computer science background more effectively. This continuous learning process allows me to offer innovative and up-to-date solutions for my clients, ensuring they stay ahead in a competitive digital landscape.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I look forward to the opportunity to share my passion for hospitality and technology with you and to help create many more unforgettable experiences and successful business ventures.
